The question you're thinking

“Why wouldn’t we
just build this?”

Because when the IRS audits a $40M charitable tax deduction, Uber needs an independent third party that can verify every cent reached a verified food bank. That’s not an engineering problem — it’s a compliance problem. DimeTech handles the fund routing, verification, audit trail, and tax reporting so Uber doesn’t have to.

IRS requires substantiation for charitable deductions over $250 (IRC §170(f)(8)). Third-party verification strengthens audit defense.
