How DimeTech works

An automatic addition.
Not a customer donation.

11¢ is built into every transaction. It’s not a round-up. It’s not a surcharge. It’s part of the price — and it becomes Uber’s charitable donation, with appropriate tax deductions.

Because most people turn down “round up,” “contribute the change,” and “add a dollar” — we designed DimeAid to be built into every transaction. 100% participation.

The price is $18.84. 10¢ is Uber’s donation to certified charities — and therefore Uber’s tax deduction. 1¢ funds DimeTech’s verification and operations.

Funds flow through DimeTech, then disbursed to certified charities on a weekly/monthly basis. Tax receipts generated on the same schedule. Subject to legal review under Canadian Income Tax Act.
