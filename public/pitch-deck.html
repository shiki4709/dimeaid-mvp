The thesis

The only giving model that survives is one where the donation is invisible to the buyer and invisible to AI.

That’s DimeAid. An 11¢ verified donation embedded into every transaction. Partner-funded. Tax-deductible. 100% participation by default.

How it works

One integration.
Everything else is automatic.

01
Connect your payment system
API or webhook. Works with Stripe, Square, Toast, Clover, or any POS. No frontend changes.
02
Every transaction generates a donation
Each completed sale triggers a verified $0.10 donation to a registered food bank. Amount, recipient, and receipt ID are logged per transaction.
03
Consolidated tax receipts are issued quarterly
One receipt per quarter from a CRA-registered charity. Deductible under ITA s.110.1. Audit-ready documentation included.
