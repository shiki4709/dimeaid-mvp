Key Takeaways

Uber makes money on this. With even a 0.5% cause marketing uplift, the program is net positive for Uber — they earn $7.35M MORE than it costs. The charitable impact is free.

Partners make money too. Offering DimeAid free to restaurants gives them their own tax deduction. The match promotion doubles the impact at zero cost to Uber.

DimeTech makes money. 1¢ × 1B transactions = $10M/year in operations revenue.
